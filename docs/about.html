Hello! My name is Joanna Sanchez, and I recently graduated from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign with a Bachelor's degree in Molecular & Cellular Biology.

My academic journey has been shaped by perseverance, curiosity, and a commitment to personal growth. Through my experiences in research, I have developed a deep appreciation for the critical thinking, problem-solving, and analytical approaches that drive scientific discovery. What excites me most about research is the opportunity to ask meaningful questions, explore complex problems, and contribute to knowledge that can have a lasting impact.

These experiences have strengthened my passion for collaborative learning and scientific exploration.

As I begin the next stage of my career, I am seeking opportunities in research and laboratory environments where I can continue developing my skills, contribute to meaningful scientific projects, and learn from interdisciplinary teams. While my background is rooted in biology, I am broadly interested in research that values curiosity, critical thinking, innovation, and discovery.
