I formulated an academic plan for Undergrad students to learn more about immuniology created activities for a deeper understanding of immunology and its importance. The activities focus on creating an interactive activities that contain videos(see an example), a short story, and other resources. Ultimately, creating an opportunity for an undergraduate research program at the Community College level. Our goal is to spark some students interest through these activities and participate in our Immunolgy Epitope Database(IEDB) research. I utilized IEDB to research Sars-Cov-2 and focused on Delta and Omicron strains. We focused in gathering data on its mutations and epitopes of each strain with Undergraduate Students.
