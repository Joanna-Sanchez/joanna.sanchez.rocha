    We will strive for gender, cultural, sexual orientation, economic, political, racial, and social equity within the Chicanx community. The main focus of MEChA is to bring awareness concerning the problems of discrimination and other obstacles that confront us in our endeavor to succeed in higher education. MEChA seeks to promote Chicanx unity and empowerment through political action by uplifting and mobilizing Chicanx students through higher education, furthering cultural and Chicanx identity. We will support the struggle of other progressive movements on our campus and in our communities internationally.

    TSP is inspired by our own struggles as Latinx students pursuing higher education. The Sideline Project has a mission to facilitate, encourage and support Latinx students who like us, wish to further their education. We are advocates for our community and a voice that will represent each and every single individual within our Latinx community regardless of age, gender, or sexual orientation.
